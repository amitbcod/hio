    Product Badges

    Yellow Markets help merchants showcase the unique value of their products.

    These badges, like "Made in Mauritius", "Social Empowerment", "Health Friendly" and "Environment Friendly", instantly communicate quality, trust, and sustainability to potential buyers. Obtaining these badges can boost your product visibility, foster customer confidence, and drive more sales by aligning your business with important buyer values such as supporting local or eco-conscious shopping.

    Be sure to review the requirements and apply for relevant badges to maximise your product's appeal!

    You can learn about Yellow Markets Product Badges within our blog section

     

    Submission Guide for "Made in Mauritius" Badge Application


    The "Made in Mauritius" badge is awarded to businesses whose products are manufactured in Mauritius and meet specific criteria. To qualify for this badge, both the products origin and the company's ownership and workforce composition must comply with the guidelines below.


    To qualify for the Made in Mauritius badge, you will need to provide the following documentation and information:

     

    Example of Eligibility and Illegibility


    Eligible Product:

    A clothing brand that makes its garments in Mauritius from locally sourced fabric.


    Ineligible Product:

    A company that only assembles imported electronic parts in Mauritius.

     

    Product Origin Requirements


    For a product to be eligible for the "Made in Mauritius" badge, it must be entirely or significantly manufactured in Mauritius. Products that are simply assembled in Mauritius from foreign components do not qualify. Additional requirements apply depending on ownership structure and workforce composition, as outlined below.

     

    Ownership and Workforce Requirements


    1. Mauritian Ownership 50%:
        The management and workforce must consist of at least 50% Mauritian nationals.


    2. Mauritian Ownership < 50%:
        The management and workforce must average at least 75% Mauritian nationals.


    3. Foreign Owner Residing in Mauritius:
        The management and workforce must consist of at least 50% Mauritian nationals.


    4. Foreign Owner Residing Outside Mauritius:
        The management team must be at least 75% Mauritian nationals, and the workforce must be minimum 90% Mauritian nationals.


    These criteria ensure that businesses receiving the badge not only produce locally but also contribute significantly to the Mauritian workforce.

     

    Required Documentation


    To support your application, you will need to provide the below mentioned documents. 


    1. Business Registration
        Upload a copy of your valid Business Registration Number (BRN) certificate as a PDF document.


    2. Proof of Ownership
        Provide either:

    • An Extract of Files from the Registrar of Companies, AND/OR
    • National Identity Cards or Residence Permits of owners/shareholders.

    3. Proof of Product Manufacturing
        Provide either:

    •  A signed declaration stating the product is manufactured in Mauritius, OR
    • The address of the factory or production site.

    4. Proof of Workforce Composition
        Provide documentation verifying the nationality of your management and workforce. Provide either:

    • A breakdown of the total workforce by position and nationality, OR
    • A HR certification confirming the workforce composition.

    5. Raw Material Sourcing or Full Manufacturing in Mauritius

    • Raw materials are sourced in Mauritius, provide:
      • Purchase orders or supplier agreements providing local sourcing.
    • Manufacturing is outsourced locally, provide:
      • A signed production agreement with local manufacturer, OR
      • Manufacturer declaration on local material sourcing.


    Support and Submission


    If you have any questions regarding the documentation or application process, please proceed by submitting your application and creating a Support Ticket through the Yellow Markets platform. Our team will be happy to assist you with any queries.

    Submission Guide for Social Empowerment" Badge Application


    To apply for the Social Empowerment" badge, businesses must demonstrate meaningful contributions to social causes and commitments to sustainability, workforce education, and gender diversity in leadership. Below are the documentation requirements for each aspect of the badge application.

    To qualify for the Social Empowerment" badge, you will need to provide the following documentation and information:

     

    Example of Eligibility and Illegibility


    Eligible Company:

    A business that regularly donates a portion of its profits to local NGOs, organises quarterly volunteering events for its employees.


    Ineligible Company:

    A business that only makes one time donations and has no ongoing social initiatives or participation from its workforce.

     

    Required Documentation

    Provide documentation for one or more below social empowerment initiatives.


    1. Contribution to Charitable or NonProfit Organisations
    Your company's contributions to charitable or nongovernmental organisations (NGOs) focused on social causes. Provide either:

    • Summary of ongoing initiatives,
      AND
      • Donation receipts, AND/OR
      • Acknowledgement letters, AND/OR
      • Partnership agreements.

    2. Workforce Education or Participation in Social and Environmental Initiatives

    Provide evidence of either workforce education OR active participation in social and environmental initiatives. At least 40% of the workforce must be actively engaged in these initiatives.

    • Signed management declaration on scope and frequency of activities, OR
    • Internal communications or participation lists confirming involvement.
    • AND
      • Training schedules and attendance lists for courses on sustainability or social responsibility, OR
      • Photos, videos, or reports from company organised events (e.g. tree planting, volunteering).

    Female Representation in Management
    Provide a report verifying at least 40% of management are women:

    • Gender breakdown (e.g., HR report or organisational chart) OR,
    • Signed certification from HR or leadership confirming gender ratio.


    Support and Submission


    If you have any questions regarding the documentation or application process, please proceed by submitting your application and creating a Support Ticket through the Yellow Markets platform. Our team will be happy to assist you with any queries.

    Submission Guide for "Environment Friendly" Badge Application


    The "Environment Friendly" badge recognises businesses that take significant steps to minimise their environmental impact through sustainable and eco friendly practices. This badge allows your company to showcase its commitment to sustainability and attract environmentally conscious consumers.


    To qualify for the "Environment Friendly" badge, you will need to provide the following documentation and information:

     

    Example of Eligibility and Illegibility


    Eligible Product:

    A furniture company using reclaimed wood from local demolition sites, powered by solar energy, and avoiding harmful chemicals.

      

    Ineligible Product:

    A toy manufacturer using imported plastic components, with no recyclable materials and harmful emissions from fossil-fueled production.

     

    Required Documentation


    1. Eco Friendliness Policies

    Provide a document or written statement outlining your company's eco friendly policies. This should include:

    • A description of your efforts to reduce environmental impact (e.g., upcycling, sourcing natural or reclaimed materials, reducing waste), OR
    • Details on how your business promotes sustainability, such as resource conservation, recycling initiatives, or steps taken to minimise carbon emissions.

    2. Proof of Sustainable Material Sourcing

    Your product is made from repurposed or naturally sourced materials without formal certification (e.g., reclaimed wood, upcycled materials), you must provide either:

    • A detailed explanation of where and how these materials are sourced, confirming their environmentally friendly nature, and how their use reduces environmental impact compared to traditional materials, OR
    • You can include photos or videos of the sourcing process, such as gathering materials from nature, conducting cleanup initiatives, or repurposing discarded items.

    3. Biodegradability/Recyclability

    Your product is biodegradable, recyclable, or made from recycled materials, provide either:

    • A product description or label that highlights its eco-friendly features (e.g., natural, biodegradable, recyclable), OR
    • A signed statement explaining how the product or its components can decompose naturally or be recycled, even if formal certifications are not available.

    4. Manufacturing Process

    Your manufacturing process reduces or eliminates the emission of harmful pollutants, provide either:

    • An Environmental Safety Report from relevant authorities or a reputable auditing firm that verifies eco friendly practices, OR
    • A written statement confirming that no harmful substances are released during production. You can also include supporting materials like photos or videos of the manufacturing setup and eco friendly practices used (e.g., waste reduction, use of eco safe chemicals).

    5. Use of Renewable Energy

    Your production process relies on renewable energy sources (e.g., solar, wind, hydroelectric), provide either:

    • A brief description of how your energy use supports eco friendly initiatives, even if informal documentation is used, AND/OR
    • Energy bills or certificates that verify the use of renewable energy in your production facility or offices.


    Support and Submission


    If you have any questions about the application process or the required documentation, please submit your application and open a Support Ticket on the Yellow Markets platform. Our support team is available to assist you with any inquiries or clarifications you may need.

    Submission Guide for "Health Friendly" Badge Application


    To apply for the "Health Friendly" badge, businesses must ensure their products meet specific criteria based on their category:


    Edible Products (Food and Beverages):

    Products must be safe for consumption and adhere to local nutrition labelling regulations, with verified nutritional values and quality control standards.


    Non-Edible Products (External Use):

    Products should meet health and safety guidelines, including the use of non-toxic, skin-safe ingredients, and comply with quality assurance protocols to ensure consumer safety.


    Note: All products, whether edible or non-edible, must not be tested on animals and should be tested for potential allergies to ensure consumer safety.

    To qualify for the "Health Friendly" badge, you will need to provide the following documentation and information:

     

    Example of Eligibility and Illegibility


    Eligible Product:
    A snack with a certified nutrition label, low sugar, and regular quality control checks.


    Ineligible Product:
    A beverage with an unverified nutrition label and no consistent quality control.

     

    Required Documentation


    1. Compliance with Local Nutrition Labelling

    • Provide a clear photo or PDF of the existing label.
    • Product should follow the New Facts Label Requirements of the U.S Food & Drug Administration (FDA) FDA Nutrition Facts Label

    2. Basic Nutrition Information
    Provide either:

    • A nutritionists certification or letter confirming high nutritional value (Score A), OR
    • A nutrition summary explaining health benefits (e.g., low sugar, high fibre).

    3. Targeted Health Benefits
    Add a short note in the product description if the product is suited for specific health conditions (e.g., diabetes, heart health).

    4. Quality Control Procedure (QC Reports or Procedure Manual)
    Provide either:

    • A copy of quality control (QC) report or logs from recent batches, OR
      A copy of your company's quality assurance, OR
      A copy of the quality control manual detailing the process.


    Support and Submission


    If you have any questions regarding the documentation or application process, please proceed by submitting your application and creating a Support Ticket through the Yellow Markets platform. Our team will be happy to assist you with any queries.
